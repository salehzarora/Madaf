# Madaf / מדף — Product Brief

## What is Madaf?

**Madaf** (Hebrew/Arabic: "shelf" — מדף / رفّ, brand transliteration مدف) is a
B2B supplier catalog and ordering platform for local goods suppliers in
Israel.

## The real-world problem

A supplier has a warehouse and a delivery van full of wholesale goods. He
visits groceries, kiosks, supermarkets and small shops. Today:

- He explains products manually and sends WhatsApp pictures.
- The shop owner can't browse the full assortment, compare categories,
  pick quantities, or produce a clean order.
- Orders arrive as voice notes, photos and scribbles — errors and missed
  items are routine.

## The Madaf solution

A modern, tablet/mobile-first web app:

1. The supplier or sales rep opens the catalog on a tablet **inside the shop**.
2. The shop owner browses products by category, manufacturer and search.
3. Products are shown clearly: image, name, package info, wholesale price,
   availability, optional expiry info.
4. Products go into a cart; a clean **order request** is created and sent.
5. The supplier receives a structured order with all details.
6. The same catalog can later be sent as a **link** for remote ordering.

## Target market & users

| User | Context | Primary language(s) |
|---|---|---|
| Supplier / owner | Back-office, warehouse | Hebrew, Arabic |
| Sales rep | In-shop tablet visits | Arabic, Hebrew |
| Shop owner | In shop or remote via link | Arabic, Hebrew |
| Admin / accountant | Documents, reports | Hebrew (documents), English fallback |

- Market: Israel / local.
- **Arabic, Hebrew and English from day one.** Arabic and Hebrew are RTL.
- Hebrew is the default for documents; Arabic must be excellent (many users
  are Arabic speakers); English is a clean fallback/admin option.

## Brand

- **Name:** Madaf / מדף / مدف
- **Feeling:** modern, trustworthy, B2B, clean, fast, tablet-friendly,
  wholesale/catalog oriented. Not childish, not overly corporate — a
  professional sales tool used by real suppliers.
- **Visual identity:** deep "shelf teal" primary (`brand-600 #1e7a70`),
  warm neutral background, white cards, warm amber accent used sparingly.
  Logo mark: a stocked shelf in a rounded square.
